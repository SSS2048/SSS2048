ABOUT US

"SSS is a registered society that was established in November,2020 by some group of students. It was set up in order to tackle the problems of poverty, especially amongst young children. From its humble origins as a small student society it has grown rapidly, and now its projects cover many of activities located in Hyderabad. SSS Society is committed to the welfare of people from the most underprivileged strata of society without distinction of caste, creed or color. Its emphasis is on assisting the children and youth who are the citizens of tomorrow and the disabled who need a support to help them join mainstream society."
